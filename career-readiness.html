Career & Employment Readiness

Equipping job seekers with the professional skills, confidence, and career strategies needed to secure employment and advance in the workforce.

Request a Free Consultation View All Programs →

Program Overview

Who This Program Serves

  • First-time job seekers and recent graduates
  • Adults re-entering the workforce after a gap
  • WIOA participants and workforce agency clients
  • Youth ages 16–24 seeking first employment
  • Individuals transitioning to new career pathways

About This Program

Getting hired requires more than a resume — it requires confidence, professional presence, and the practical skills employers expect from day one. Medley Innovation's Career & Employment Readiness program delivers hands-on, coach-led training that prepares participants for the full employment lifecycle from job search to career advancement.

Available via Zoom — all 50 states
In-person — Southern Nevada
Workshop, bootcamp & ongoing formats
Partner & agency delivery available

Curriculum

Key Topics Covered

Each module is practical, outcomes-focused, and delivered by experienced educators. Programs are customizable to meet the specific needs of your organization or participants.

📄

Resume Development

Crafting a professional, ATS-optimized resume that stands out to employers and applicant tracking systems.

🎤

Interview Preparation

Mock interviews, common question coaching, body language, and how to communicate value confidently.

👔

Workplace Professionalism

Dress, communication etiquette, punctuality, and navigating workplace culture and expectations.

🗺️

Career Pathway Coaching

Identifying career goals, mapping out pathways, and aligning skills with in-demand occupations.

🤝

Soft Skills Training

Teamwork, conflict resolution, emotional intelligence, and professional communication.

💻

Digital Literacy

Email etiquette, Microsoft Office basics, video conferencing, and online job application platforms.

📈

Job Retention Coaching

Strategies to succeed in a new role, navigate workplace challenges, and build long-term employment stability.

🚀

Career Advancement Planning

Setting goals, seeking promotions, continuing education, and building workplace relationships.
