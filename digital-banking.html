Digital & Banking Access Programs

Bridging the digital divide by equipping individuals and small businesses with the digital financial skills needed to safely navigate modern banking and payment systems.

Request a Free Consultation View All Programs →

Program Overview

Who This Program Serves

  • Unbanked and underbanked individuals gaining financial access
  • Seniors and older adults learning digital banking tools
  • Small business owners adopting digital payment systems
  • Immigrants and new Americans navigating U.S. banking
  • Low-income individuals accessing banking for the first time

About This Program

The shift to digital banking has left millions of Americans behind — particularly seniors, low-income individuals, immigrants, and those without prior banking experience. Medley Innovation's Digital & Banking Access programs bridge this gap, delivering practical, hands-on digital financial literacy education that builds confidence, safety awareness, and financial access.

Available via Zoom — all 50 states
In-person — Southern Nevada
Workshop, bootcamp & ongoing formats
Partner & agency delivery available

Curriculum

Key Topics Covered

Each module is practical, outcomes-focused, and delivered by experienced educators. Programs are customizable to meet the specific needs of your organization or participants.

💻

Online Banking Education

Setting up and managing online bank accounts, reading statements, and using online tools safely.

📱

Mobile Banking Training

Using banking apps, mobile check deposit, account alerts, and managing finances from a smartphone.

💸

Digital Payment Literacy

Understanding Zelle, Venmo, PayPal, Cash App — benefits, risks, and safe usage practices.

🛡️

Fraud Prevention & Cybersecurity

Recognizing phishing, account takeover, and digital scams — and how to protect your financial accounts.

🔐

Online Security Awareness

Strong passwords, two-factor authentication, and safe financial behavior online.

💳

Fintech Tools Education

Using budgeting apps, credit monitoring tools, and financial technology to improve financial management.

👛

Digital Wallet Education

Apple Pay, Google Pay, and contactless payment tools — how they work and how to use them safely.

🏪

Small Business Payment Systems

ACH, wire transfers, point-of-sale systems, and digital invoicing for small business owners.
